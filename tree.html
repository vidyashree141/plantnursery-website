Our Tree Varieties

Mango Tree

The Mango tree is known for its delicious fruits and vibrant green leaves.

Neem Tree

The Neem tree is highly valued for its medicinal properties and shade.

Banyan Tree

A symbol of strength, the Banyan tree is known for its massive size and longevity.

Coconut Tree

The Coconut tree provides valuable fruits and is a staple in coastal areas.

Pine Tree

Known for its evergreen nature, the Pine tree is often associated with mountains.

Teak Tree

Teak wood is prized for its durability and is used in fine furniture making.

Peepal Tree

The Peepal tree has religious significance and is a source of oxygen and shade.

Gulmohar Tree

The Gulmohar tree is known for its striking red flowers, making it a popular ornamental tree.

Apple Tree

The Apple tree is famous for its delicious fruits and beautiful blossoms.

Ashoka Tree

The Ashoka tree is associated with beauty and spirituality, often planted in gardens.
